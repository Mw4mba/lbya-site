# **4\. MCT product page: realistic overview**

The MCT page should sound ambitious but grounded. It should not promise full automation, guaranteed border clearance, guaranteed transport availability, or live tracking unless those capabilities are implemented and verified.

## **Recommended page hero**

| Element | Website copy |
| ----- | ----- |
| Page title | Malaika Control Tower |
| Tagline | Transport and logistics control for connected African trade. |
| Hero paragraph | MCT helps logistics teams bring order, trust, and visibility to complex transport coordination. It turns scattered requests, transporter details, truck documents, quotes, status updates, and decisions into a structured control workflow designed for African logistics operations.  |
| Primary CTA | Request early access |
| Secondary CTA | Book a product discussion. |

## **What MCT is**

MCT is a practical control platform for transport coordination and logistics in the African market. It helps teams replace scattered communication with a single, structured way to manage requests, transporters, truck documents, quotes, communication, shipment status, and decision evidence.

The result is a more professional coordination environment where each request, transporter, truck, document, quote, status update, and decision can be tracked, reviewed, and trusted with greater confidence.

## **What MCT is not**

* It is not a replacement for transport companies, freight forwarders, customs authorities, insurers, or legal due diligence.  
* It should not publicly claim to guarantee truck availability, customs clearance, border crossing success, payment security, or live GPS visibility unless those features are formally implemented.  
* It is not presented as a full ERP, accounting system, or global transport management system from day one.  
* It should be presented as a focused control platform that can grow through integrations, partnerships, and enterprise workflows.

## **Who MCT is for**

* **Cargo owners and clients** who need clearer visibility and structure when requesting transport.  
* **Transport coordinators** who manage quotes, trucks, documents, and operational communication.  
* **Transporters and fleet partners** who need a more professional way to present availability, documents, and service status.  
* **Freight and logistics partners** working across African corridors and cross-border operations.  
* **African market operators** who need stronger coordination and evidence when moving goods.  
* **LBYA internal operations** for managing verified transport packs, evidence, and client-facing coordination.

## **Problems MCT addresses**

| Common problem | MCT response |
| ----- | ----- |
| Transport requests are scattered across WhatsApp, email, calls, and spreadsheets. | MCT structures requests into a controlled workflow with statuses, contacts, documents, and actions. |
| Truck and transporter documents are difficult to verify and organise.  | MCT keeps transporter profiles, truck packs, document status, expiry dates, and verification evidence in one place. |
| Quote negotiation and margin management are hard to track. | MCT supports organised quote comparisons, offer history, and internal coordination without exposing sensitive margin logic publicly. |
| There is no clear operational status. | MCT provides a dashboard that shows the request stage, document status, assigned responsibility, and coordination progress. |
| Evidence is lost in chats and folders. | MCT keeps a record of documents, checks, decisions, and references to communications for later review. |
| Clients need trust, but the process is often informal. | MCT creates a more professional and traceable control environment around transport coordination. |

# **6\. MCT package structure without prices**

The website should show packages to make the product easier to understand, but prices should not be displayed yet. Use “Contact us,” “Request early access,” or “Custom quote” instead of pricing.

## **Package positioning**

| Package | Best for | Positioning |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| MCT Basic | Small teams and early users that need a simple starting point for capturing requests, storing documents, and replacing informal coordination.  | Start with the essentials: one place to register transport requests, store key documents, track basic status, and create a more professional workflow without added operational complexity.  |
| MCT Professional | Growing logistics coordinators, transport brokers, or companies managing multiple users, transporters, trucks, documents, and quote workflows.  | Move from basic organisation to operational control: assign responsibilities, manage transporter and truck records, monitor document gaps, compare quotes, and keep evidence of key checks.  |
| MCT Premium | Established teams that need management visibility, partner collaboration, risk control, reporting, and more guided workflows across active operations.  | Scale coordination with stronger oversight: controlled client or partner access, risk flags, priority document review, management dashboards, custom reports, and integration-ready data exports.  |
| MCT Enterprise | Large organisations, multi-country operations, institutional clients, and strategic partners with complex needs in governance, integration, security, or rollout.  | Configure MCT around enterprise operations: tailored workflows, approval levels, multi-region structures, integration planning, executive reporting, dedicated implementation, and enhanced security options.  |

## **MCT Basic**

For small teams that need the quickest path from informal coordination to a clean, structured workflow. Basic focuses on capturing transport requests, storing essential documents, following simple statuses, and creating a more reliable operating routine.

* Transport request registration  
* Basic client and transporter records  
* Document upload and storage  
* Manual verification checklist  
* Basic request status tracking  
* Simple dashboard for active requests  
* Exportable summary report  
* Standard support

## **MCT Professional**

For growing operations that need to coordinate more people, transporters, trucks, documents, and quotes with accountability. Professional adds team responsibility, structured transporter and truck records, stronger document control, quote comparison, and evidence logs.

* Everything in Basic  
* Multi-user workspace and team responsibilities  
* Transporter and truck database  
* Truck-pack document control  
* Document expiry and missing-information alerts  
* Quote comparison and offer coordination workflow  
* Evidence log for checks and status changes  
* More detailed dashboard and exportable reports  
* Role-based access for internal users

## **MCT Premium**

For established teams that need management visibility, partner collaboration, risk control, and more guided workflows. Premium supports controlled portal access, advanced status views, priority document review, risk flags, custom reports, and integration-ready exports.

* Everything in Professional  
* Controlled client or partner portal options  
* Advanced request and trip-status views  
* Rule-based workflow status and reminders  
* Priority document review workflow  
* Risk flags for missing, expired, or unverified documents  
* Advanced dashboards for management oversight  
* Custom report templates  
* Priority support and onboarding session  
* API-ready export structure for future integrations

## **MCT Enterprise**

For large or strategic clients that need MCT adapted to their operating model. Enterprise focuses on tailored workflows, multi-country or multi-business-unit structures, approval levels, integration planning, executive reporting, dedicated implementation, training, and enhanced security options.

* Everything in Premium  
* Custom workflow configuration  
* Multi-country or multi-business-unit setup  
* Advanced user roles and approval levels  
* Integration planning with ERP, TMS, accounting, telematics, or external verification sources where technically available  
* Custom dashboards and executive reporting  
* Dedicated implementation support  
* Training and change-management package  
* Service-level agreement options  
* Private environment or enhanced security options to be evaluated per client need

# **13\. Website copy rules and claims to avoid**

## **Use this language**

* Control platform  
* Coordination workflow  
* Verification support  
* Document control  
* Evidence and traceability  
* Decision readiness  
* Pilot phase / early access  
* Designed to integrate with existing tools  
* Product-led technology company

## **Avoid this language unless formally true**

* Guaranteed customs clearance  
* Guaranteed transporter availability  
* Fully automated logistics platform  
* Certified compliance platform  
* Real-time GPS tracking if not integrated  
* Official government verification unless connected to an official source  
* Replaces Autodesk, Solibri, BIMcollab, transporters, or freight forwarders  
* MCT is 90 per cent ready on the public website

| Better public wording Instead of saying “MCT is 90 per cent ready,” say: “MCT is entering its early-access and pilot phase.” This sounds more professional and protects the product image.  |
